What is this tool for?

Long gone are the days of writing your name on a whiteboard, and hoping that a TA would eventually show up.

This tool lets you sign up for TA help during scheduled labs and office hours. Labs in CPSC 210 operate on a first-come, first-served basis, however we will give priority to students who have not received help from a course staff member yet.

To join the queue, please fill the form on this page.
